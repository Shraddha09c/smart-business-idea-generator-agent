# 🚀 Smart Business Idea Generator Agent

An Agentic AI-powered business innovation assistant that helps users generate creative startup ideas, refine business concepts, and receive personalized entrepreneurial guidance using IBM watsonx Orchestrate and IBM Granite Models.

---

### Overview

Smart Business Idea Generator Agent is an AI-powered web application developed as part of the **IBM SkillsBuild Workshop**. The application assists students, entrepreneurs, and aspiring innovators in generating business ideas based on their interests, business goals, and target audience.

Instead of relying on traditional brainstorming, users can interact with an intelligent AI assistant to receive startup suggestions, business models, implementation guidance, and idea refinement in real time.

---

### Problem Statement

Generating innovative and practical business ideas can be challenging for students and entrepreneurs. Brainstorming sessions are often unstructured, while identifying market opportunities and selecting suitable business models requires significant research and analysis.

Smart Business Idea Generator Agent addresses this challenge by:

- Generating innovative startup ideas using AI
- Providing personalized business recommendations
- Refining existing business concepts
- Suggesting business models and implementation strategies
- Offering an interactive conversational AI experience
